About Us

Yeye Tech Consults Ltd is a premier investment consulting firm in Uganda, dedicated to guiding both local and foreign investors through the dynamic landscape of doing business in Uganda and across Africa.

With a team of seasoned professionals—including international business experts, investor relations specialists, human resource strategists, business administrators, and commercial lawyers—we provide comprehensive advisory services tailored to maximize investment success.

Why Invest in Africa?

Political & Economic Stability

A business-friendly environment supported by government incentives.

Rich Natural Resources

From fertile agricultural land to untapped oil and mineral reserves.

Young & Skilled Workforce

A growing labor force ready to power industries and innovation.

Regional Market Access

Gateway to the East African Community (EAC) and the African Continental Free Trade Area (AfCFTA).
